Atal Utkrisht Government Inter College Dhokaney, Nainital


In Ramgarh Block of District Nainital, a little ahead from “Awakening Tabsthali of Swami Vivekanand -Kakrighat and before ‘Almora City’ stands a beautiful place Dhokaney. This nice place is only 3 kms. away from ‘Suyalbari Bazar’, which is located at ‘Haldwani-Almora’ National Highway and by the side of constant ‘Koshi river’.

Amidst the beauty of mountains along motor road side Utkrisht Government Inter College Dhokaney is situated. All around this pleasant college Pine, Oak, Buransh, Mapple and Utis trees make it’s environment delightful. Right in front of the college building across rivulet we can view an unique attraction of paddy-Pottato field, high mountains, dark blue sky and lush greenery. Also we look a beautiful waterfall is flowing over the edge of a steep cliff along with it’s milky image. Really a magnificent view stores in mind. Chirping birds. romping animals, blooming flowers and sweet sensation of kids inevitably make this place wonderful.

Atal Utkrisht Government Inter College Dhokaney was established in 1975 by Utter Pradesh Government. Before it’s establishment some reputed people of local area were conducting this educational body with their own efforts

Presently this Inter College is a glaring light for a broad range of Dhokaney- Suyalbari zone. Since it’s establishment this educational body has become an originator of Doctors, engineers, high class officers, teachers, scientists and brave army persons. For recognizance, this is notable most students of this college come from below poverty line families. They are laborers, farmers, craftmen ……etc. But these students have a strong will power to lift them up. Highly educated, laborious and well-disciplined teachers are providing them mighty knowledge in each subject. The scholar teachers of Inter College Dhokaney are preparing these students to go ahead in their life. They are doing all their bests for the betterment for poor students.

Hitherto, Atal Utkrisht Government Inter College Dhokaney, Nainital is under ‘Vidyalyi Sikshya of Uttrakhand’ but has been affiliated to CBSE Board since 2021. Co-Education classes of this college from class 6th to 12th are running in English Medium. Hindi Medium is only a choice of student. Separate, well equipped classrooms, Laboratories for science subjects and well decorated library is available. Study of computer and yoga is necessary for every one. The college has two play ground and a big hall. The school administration organize daily prayer for students and cultural programs etc. Mostly games, competitions, quizzes and other activities are being organized by college as per it’s need.

Well trained teachers are doing their work under the leadership and guidance of Principal Mr. B.K. Singh. More then300 students are getting their education, Knowledge and proper way from scholar teachers. Each department of college specially N.S.S., Examination, M.D.M., Principal Office, College Office……. etc. works in a right direction distinctively. Students get free Text Books, Scholarship and other benefits too. Moreover able students get their benefits in a large measure according their ability. In every aspect the role of College staff is just like a torch bearer. Atal Utkrisht Government Inter College Dhokaney is an excellent and a first rate educational body of the area. Students must come here to learn and serve Mother India. Indeed under the leadership of college principal Mr. B.K.Singh Atal Utkrisht Inter College Dhokaney is progressing. Come for better education at Atal Utkrisht Inter College Dhokaney. Read, Learn, C o-operate and go thoughtout the world to serve Humanity.
